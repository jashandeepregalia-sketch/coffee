Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

Who is Elite Core Media NOT for?
+
If you aren't an expert at what you do, don't have an online business, or don't have a way to convert interested viewers — we're just not the right fit. However, if you have an online business and believe you're one of the best in your field, you will not find a more profitable way to scale your content outside of working with us.
What results can I expect?
+
This highly depends on what kind of client you are. Worst case, you try but don't go all in, and you walk away with a 1–1.5x increase on the content you came in with. Best case, you implement everything and see results like our top clients — $30K to $188K/month in 88 days with zero paid ads. On average, most clients see a 2x–3x views increase on the low end, 10x–20x on the high end, and have already recouped their investment within the first month.
How much does it cost?
+
We're one of the most premium content agencies in the market, and our pricing reflects that. If your priority is affordability, we'd suggest another option. If your priority is the best results in the fastest timeframe while building a brand that exudes quality — you'll love what we present on the call.
What platforms do you help with?
+
We create content across Instagram, TikTok, Facebook, YouTube Shorts, LinkedIn, and more. Our primary focus is platforms best for client acquisition, but great videos perform everywhere — no need for different content per platform.
Do I need a big audience to begin?
+
No. Audience size is never the issue — it's always about the client. As long as you're an expert, content is a priority, and you have an offer to monetize, Elite Core Media works regardless of where you're starting from.
Do you have a guarantee?
+
The best way to answer this is to watch our work and client results. We're the most premium option in the market. If you resonate with our approach, let's talk. If affordability is the priority right now, we'd suggest looking elsewhere until you're in a place to upgrade — no hard feelings.
Will this work for my niche?
+
Yes. We've worked with business owners across every niche imaginable and the niche is never the issue. As long as you're an expert in what you do, content is a priority, and you have an offer to monetize — Elite Core Media will work for your niche.
